Photos from my last vacation


A sunny day in Rome, Italy.

Impressions from Rome, Italy on a sunny day. A Sunday stroll ended in an unexpected discussion with two local Italians about the upcoming elections. Two espressi later, the verdict was clear: Europe must stay united.

Living in Europe is great. It offers so many opportunities. One day I'm in Spain,the next morning I'm all the way up in Poland. I don't usually consider myself a cosmopolitan, but I do feel European.

Trevi fountain

The fountain was built at the end of an aqueduct (Aqua Virgo) that was constructed in 19BC. The Aqua Virgo was built by Augustus's son-in-law Agrippa to supply water for the Roman Baths. The water for the fountain comes from the Salone Springs about 14 miles outside of Rome..

Rumour has it that a thief called Giovanni di Lorenzo comes every night to collect the coins in the fountain. A smart man if you ask me.
